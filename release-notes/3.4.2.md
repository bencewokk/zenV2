# Zen 3.4.2 — Aurora

## Your phone, on the dashboard

- The "Zen on your phone" tile now becomes a live feed once a phone is linked: open tasks captured on the go show up right on the dashboard, tickable in place, with due-date labels and notes. Ticks sync back to the phone.
- A **Show QR code** button on the feed brings the pairing QR back any time — for another device or a re-install.
- New First Run Path goal under Connect Real Life: **Link your phone via the QR tile**, with a walkthrough step that shows you the QR and ticks itself once your phone's data first syncs.

## Polish

- Codenames are now tied to the major version — the whole 3.x line is **Aurora**; a new word arrives with 4.0.
- The release-notes card no longer repeats the version heading in its summary line.
