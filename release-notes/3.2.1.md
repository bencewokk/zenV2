# Zen 3.2.1

## Your look owns the accent

- Zen, Veil, and Orb each bring their own accent color — Zen stays blue, Veil turns violet, Orb goes cyan. Everything follows: buttons, tabs, links, readiness bars, and the dashboard highlights that used to stay stubbornly blue.
- The separate accent picker is gone — pick a look and the palette is handled.

## Eight fonts

- Settings → Appearance now offers Inter, Geist, IBM Plex Sans, Literata, Newsreader, Atkinson Hyperlegible, and JetBrains Mono alongside the system default — all bundled with the app, no downloads.

## Tighter header

- The top bar is slimmer, the expanded menu no longer leaves empty space under the cards, session tabs never show a scrollbar, and the window buttons now live inside the header.

## Safer testing

- **Export full backup** in Settings → Data saves your notes, Deep Work sessions, quizzes, memory, and settings to one file — and **Restore backup** brings them back without deleting anything.
- **Copy diagnostics** puts a paste-able report (version, platform, recent errors — never note content) on your clipboard for bug reports.
