“will I be ready by my goal date?”

- Lesson slide batches explicitly declare whether more material is needed. Zen
  asks the tutor for another focused batch when appropriate, but final batches
  close locally instead of looping through the AI.
- Finishing a class closes its Deep Work board, preserves it for later, ends its
  owned timer, and reliably credits focused time.
- Goal forecasts now adjust mastery for evidence quality and overdue reviews,
  account for concept scope and retention work, and compare required study time
  with booked sessions and realistic daily capacity.
- Forecasts show reliable readiness, projected readiness, evidence coverage,
  workload, capacity, and clearer Ahead / On track / At risk / Overcommitted
  verdicts.
