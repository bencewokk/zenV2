# Zen 3.4.5 — Aurora

## A walkthrough that cannot lose your place

- Tutorial progress now follows the walkthrough itself instead of trying to infer what happened from notes, sessions, settings, or background state.
- Every meaningful walkthrough step has exactly one matching checklist tick; intro and completion cards stay out of the checklist.
- Advancing, completing, or explicitly skipping a step records it permanently, while going back or closing the walkthrough does not claim progress.
- The checklist is generated directly from the walkthrough definitions, so extra goals without a real step can no longer appear.
- The two-minute core loop records progress against the same checklist as the longer First Run Path.

## Smoother guided actions

- Sample notes and the seeded Deep Work session no longer interfere with the walkthrough's action gates.
- Planned-study guidance waits for a genuinely completed planned block, and appearance setup handles both look and font choices together.
- AI email-label setup can now be skipped cleanly when mail is not connected.
