# Zen 3.1.1

## Choose your app look

- Adds an **App look** picker in Settings → Appearance with three looks.
- **Zen** keeps the original graphite surfaces and aurora while the AI works.
- **Veil** retints the app near-black violet with an ink-wash veil during AI activity.
- **Orb** retints the app deep-space blue with a plasma orb during AI activity.
- Each look retints the whole shell and home surfaces, and switches live without a restart.
