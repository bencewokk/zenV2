This release makes onboarding interactive and repairs PDF sync across devices.

- Added one-click Google sign-in and sync to onboarding, fixed its Connect
  button, and replaced screenshots with working feature demos.
- Fixed PDFs syncing only their title and table of contents while leaving the
  actual document blank on the receiving device.
- PDF files now upload and download in bounded chunks, avoiding serverless
  payload limits for larger documents.
- Interrupted and previously broken PDF transfers retry automatically. The PDF
  viewer now explains when bytes are missing and offers a manual retry.
